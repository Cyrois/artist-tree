1. **Search Input** - Type to search, press Enter or click Search
2. **Filters Button** - Toggle filter panel
3. **Genre Pills** - Multi-select genre filtering
4. **Score Range** - Min/max inputs
5. **Sort Dropdown** - Score, Name, or Listeners
6. **Compact Artist Cards** - Click to view artist detail
7. **Similar Artists** - Appears after search with related artists
8. **Trending Artists** - Always visible, sorted by listeners
